## 12 · FAQ
- Eyebrow: QUESTIONS / Headline: Everything agents ask us.
1. **What photos do I need?** The ones you already have. Eight to thirty standard listing photos—wide room shots work best. Any photographer, any camera, straight from your MLS export. No special capture, no re-shoot.
2. **How long does a tour take to generate?** Most films render in under a minute; complex estates with 30+ photos may take a few. You can queue an entire portfolio and every film renders in parallel.
3. **Can I control the style and the camera?** Yes. Choose pacing, mood, music, and which rooms lead. Don't love a shot? Regenerate just that room—unlimited re-generations are included.
4. **Is it compliant for MLS and advertising?** Films depict only your uploaded photography—no invented rooms or fixtures—and export with unbranded MLS-safe versions alongside your branded cuts. Music is fully licensed for listing promotion.
5. **Who owns the films?** You do, permanently. Every film you generate is yours to publish anywhere—MLS, portals, social, paid ads—even if you later cancel.
