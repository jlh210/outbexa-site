# Outbexa — Conversion Audit & Copy Rebuild (v2)
Positioning shift: **"an AI video generator" → "the luxury listing marketing platform for modern real estate professionals."**
Every rewrite below is live on the site. Audit first, rewrite second, per section.

---

## The one structural problem (before the sections)
The current page's spine is **mechanism**: AI → automation → 72 hours → cheaper than a videographer. That framing invites the wrong comparison set (other AI tools, and "free" — doing nothing) and the wrong identity (early adopter buying a gadget). The rebuild's spine is **identity + outcome**: *Average Realtor → Luxury Marketing Realtor*, and every section answers "how does this win me listings and commissions?" Price-vs-videographer survives in exactly one place (pricing, as an anchor) instead of five.

---

## 1 · Meta
**Audit:** "Automated Luxury Video Tours" leads with the mechanism ("automated" is a commodity word — nobody brags about hiring the automated option).
**Rewrite:**
- Title: *Outbexa — Luxury Listing Marketing for Real Estate Professionals*
- Description: *Turn the listing photos you already have into cinematic property films that win listing appointments, captivate buyers, and sell homes faster. Every format. Delivered in 72 hours.*

## 2 · Hero
**Audit:** "Luxury video tours, automated for any space" describes the product's category, not the visitor's ambition. The trust line sells a discount — discounts and luxury cannot share a hero. StoryBrand: the agent is the hero; Outbexa is the guide.
**Rewrite:**
- Eyebrow: *LUXURY LISTING MARKETING · RESIDENTIAL & COMMERCIAL*
- H1: *Market every listing / **like it's worth millions.***
- Sub: *Outbexa turns the photos you already have into a cinematic property film — the kind buyers stop for, sellers talk about, and competitors can't match. Delivered in 72 hours.*
- CTAs: *Book a Call* / *Watch a Sample Tour*
- Trust: *For agents, teams, brokers & developers — residential and commercial*

## 3 · Film beats
**Audit:** The approach beat quotes videographer prices 8 seconds into a luxury experience — it breaks the spell to talk about money. The callouts describe software behavior ("reads your floor plan"). Keep the two best lines on the page ("Buyers don't scroll past this. / Neither did you." and "This is the moment that sells the home") — they already sell outcome.
**Rewrite (in scroll order):**
- Approach: *Buyers don't fall for photos. / **They fall for the feeling of walking through.*** — sub: *Every second of this film came from ordinary listing photos. Imagine your next listing doing this.*
- Meta pill: *AN OUTBEXA FILM · 0:12* (was "FILMED BY OUTBEXA AI")
- Interior sub: *Made from the photos you already have. No crew. No shoot day. No waiting.*
- Callout 1: kicker *TRUE-TO-LAYOUT WALKTHROUGHS* — *Buyers tour it **the way they'd walk it.*** — *Your floor plan guides the camera room to room, so buyers experience the real home — not a slideshow.*
- Callout 2: kicker *SIGNATURE GRADE* — *Every room, **perfectly lit.*** — *Cinematic grading on every shot. All your listings share one signature look — yours.*
- Chips: **72h** FROM PHOTOS TO FILM · **1** UPLOAD, EVERY PLATFORM · **Yours** FOREVER, UNLIMITED USE
- Stair callout: kicker *ONE UPLOAD* — *The whole home, **one take.*** — *Photos and floor plan in. A continuous cinematic walkthrough out — plus every cut you need for Instagram, TikTok, MLS, and your website.*
- Finale: *Your next listing / **could look like this.*** → Book a Call
- Guide HUD final line: *Tour complete. Yours takes 72 hours.*
- Viewfinder: *OUTBEXA · SIGNATURE GRADE · GOLDEN HOUR*

## 7 · Features → Outcomes
**Audit:** Biggest gap on the page. Five of six cards described the software. Hormozi: nobody buys the drill.
**Rewrite:** H2: *The unfair advantage, **on every listing.*** Intro: *Six ways a cinematic tour earns its commission.*
1. **Win the listing appointment** — Walk in with a cinematic film of the seller's home already made. The presentation is over before it starts.
2. **Look like the luxury option** — Signature cinematic marketing on every listing — the $400k condo and the $4M estate.
3. **Stop the scroll** — Motion holds attention where photos get swiped past: Instagram, TikTok, and the MLS.
4. **Faster to market** — Delivered within 72 hours. List this week, look like a brand by the weekend.
5. **One upload, every platform** — Vertical for Reels, TikTok and Stories. Horizontal for MLS and your website.
6. **A team behind you** — Book a call and talk through your listings with a real person. This isn't a self-serve black box.

## 10 · Pricing
**Audit:** The intro led with the competitor's price — anchoring down. Luxury pricing states its own terms first; the videographer comparison becomes the *second* sentence, as an anchor, not the headline.

- Plan name: *The Listing Marketing Plan* — $389/mo
- Sub: *Up to five cinematic property tours a month — about $78 per listing. Yours forever, unlimited usage.*
- Bullets: Up to 5 cinematic tours every month / Vertical cuts for Instagram, TikTok & Stories / Horizontal cuts for MLS & your website / Delivered within 72 hours / Own every film forever — unlimited usage rights
- CTA: *Get Started — $389/mo* · secondary: *Prefer to talk first? Book a discovery call*
- Fine print: *More than 5 listings a month? Ask us on your call. · Cancel anytime. Your films stay yours.*
